About the Mill

Behind the mill, by the river, is our Franklin Cider Mill Community Spot. On Saturdays and Sundays, Live Mellow Jazz by John Davis provides a relaxing and
fun atmosphere. Stand-up tables and seating are available
by the river where you can enjoy watching and feeding the ducks. Cider and other products are sold at our Upper and Lower Level Sales areas. Check out
Franklin Favorites, the Apple Shack, and our Tent for unique Michigan products. Dont forget to visit our Water Wheel and see the history of Franklin Cider Mill!

Experience the
Franklin Village Community

The Franklin Cider Mill believes that it is important to coexist peacefully with all our neighbors. Our intention is to encourage the growth and prosperity of our historic community.

As a seasonal business and as an attraction that brings new people to Franklin Village, we support expanding the knowledge of the Village's rich heritage along with encouraging patronage at Franklin Village's unique shops!

Food, Family and Fun

  • ONLINE ORDERING

    Please place online orders by 5:30 PM, to be picked up by 6 pm on the porch in front of the Mill.

  • FRANKLIN TENT

    Our tremendously popular Franklin Tent is filled with your favorite Michigan homemade jams, maple syrup, honey, salsas, meats, cheeses and more! Look for our 'best' Charcuterie board products to please your autumn and holiday guests! The lower sales area is completely accessible from the lower parking lot.

  • APPLE SHACK

    The Apple Shack will continue to sell a fresh selection of seasonal apples by the ½ peck (6 lbs) and bushel (40 lbs). Follow us on Instagram and Facebook to see what varieties are available each week. Our Apple Varieties and Uses Poster which includes a list of 25 apple varieties is available at our Apple Shack and to download. Learn more about their flavors, availability, and best uses for eating, salads, sauces, baking, and pies. Ask about our updated Franklin Cider Mill Apple Recipe booklet.

  • Hot Dogs and Cider
    Dogs are BACK!

    Our regular hot dogs and famous Cider Dogs (fresh hot dogs marinated in cider) will be sold on the lower level.

  • CIDER MAKING PROCESS

    The Franklin Cider Mill is open for visitors to view the amazing cider making process. You'll see the chopped apples dropping onto a pressing cloth, then being wrapped in the cloth, layered, and finally pressed to make Franklin Cider Mills' award-winning apple cider. Learn More

  • Weekends: Mellow Mill Jazz, Chris the Magician and
    Face Painting

    Behind The Mill by the river is our Franklin Cider Mill Community Spot. Enjoy Mellow Jazz on Saturdays and Sundays. Play in the bubbles, dance the afternoon away, or just enjoy the ambience. Be sure to check our social media for updates on Chris the Magician appearances and face painting.

  • HANDICAP ACCESSIBILITY

    A ramped door is available to enter the front of The Mill sales area and to view the cider making process, while the lower level is completely accessible from the lower parking lot.

  • Pet Friendly

    The Franklin Cider Mill is a pet-friendly enterprise. Keeping pets on a leash inside and outside The Mill is key to sustaining our pet-friendly approach.
